Get to know Us!

Powering Progress Since 1994

At Adroit Power Systems, power isn’t just a business—it’s our legacy. Founded in 1994 by visionary directors Mr. Shanth Kumar and Mrs. Vimala Shanth Kumar, Adroit began its journey manufacturing LT transformers for stabilizer and UPS industries. What started as a focused venture quickly evolved into one of the most trusted names in power conditioning solutions.

Today, Adroit is synonymous with reliability and performance, offering a comprehensive portfolio that includes Servo Stabilizers, Isolation Transformers, and Static Stabilizers. Our products power critical operations across India, the Middle East, and Africa, ensuring that industries and infrastructures run without disruption due to power inconsistencies.

Why Choose Adroit?

At Adroit, we understand that choosing a power solution provider is about more than just technical specifications—it's about trust, consistency, and lasting value.

With nearly three decades of industry leadership, we've built a reputation for being a dependable OEM partner to esteemed brands like Consul Neowatt (now Fuji Electric) and Hykon, supporting numerous large-scale projects across the nation.

Our attention to quality is unwavering. From our 100% load testing protocols to our ability to tailor solutions for diverse environments—including the delivery of 320 outdoor-type oil-cooled servo stabilizers for unmanned telecom towers in Nigeria—we go beyond expectations to engineer resilience into every unit we produce.

Through our partnership with TSi Power India, we also bring next-generation static stabilizers to high-precision industries that demand cutting-edge voltage regulation technology.

Powering a Reliable Future

At Adroit Power Systems, we believe that power is not just about electricity—it’s about enabling progress. It’s about ensuring that businesses run without disruption, that industries achieve maximum productivity, and that power solutions are future-ready.

A New Era: Embracing Solar and Renewable Energy

Recognizing the need for sustainable solutions in a changing world, Adroit is expanding into the solar and renewable energy space. With the same commitment to reliability and excellence that defines our core business, we now offer smart, efficient solar technologies to empower homes, businesses, and industries with clean energy.

This new chapter underscores our belief that power is not just about electricity—it’s about enabling progress while protecting the planet for future generations.

Our Vision

"To provide the most efficient, reliable, and innovative power solutions across industries, ensuring seamless operations and empowering a more sustainable future."

Adroit incepted in 1994 is based in Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, INDIA. With our founder and principal Mr.Shanth Kumar at the helm, we grew from a humble start up and established remarkable presence and name in the power management industry in South India.
